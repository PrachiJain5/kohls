What’s your vice?

Turn your something into nothing

Are you too connected? Hitting the drive-through more than the gym? Having too much fun on the weekend? Spending…worrying…working too much?

Everyone struggles with something. In fact, in a recent survey on consumer attitudes about health, 46% of respondents said they’re concerned that unhealthy habits will catch up with them. Yet only 54% said they do all they can to be healthy.* No wonder it’s said that perfection is a moving target!

Before you give yourself a pass for not being perfect, consider this: Your something may not be a big deal now, but it can impact how you feel, how much you’re spending and lots of other things over time.
