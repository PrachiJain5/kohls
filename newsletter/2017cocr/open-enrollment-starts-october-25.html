FALL 2017 ISSUE

Open Enrollment starts October 25

5 things to know now!

Open Enrollment is your annual opportunity to check out your benefit options and make choices that will help you live the life you love. Designing a life you love is about making the most of what Kohl’s has to offer—and Kohl’s offers an array of benefits that support all aspects of your total well-being.

Here are 5 things to know now:

1

Save the date: Open Enrollment for your 2018 benefits starts October 25 and runs through November 8, 2017.

2

Experience myHR: As in past years, you’ll continue to view your benefits information and enroll online. However, the website has a new name: myHR, which reflects its new role as the Kohl’s HR Portal. It’s got a new URL too: myHR.kohls.com. When you visit the site to enroll, you’ll also have access to wide array of HR information, tools and resources. Be sure to add myHR.kohls.com to your favorites list for easy access in the future.

3

Check out the 2018 online enrollment guide:  New and improved for 2018, this year’s e-Guide is your one-stop resource for info about your 2018 benefit options. We’ll send you an email the evening of October 17 with a link to the online guide.

4

Let Sara help you: This year, we’re offering a new online benefits enrollment guidance tool. It’s called Sara and it’s designed to make it even easier for you to compare your medical plan choices.

5

Action is required: Make your choices. You must enroll by November 8, 2017 if you want benefits coverage for 2018. The coverage you selected for 2017 will not carry over to 2018.

Want more time in your day?

Save time by enrolling online. The process is fast and easy!
