Know where to go

Get the right care at the right time and place.

Need to see a doctor but not sure where to start? Take a look at your options, so you’ll know where to go for care when you need it.

Start with your doc

Your primary care doctor

If your condition isn’t urgent, your primary doctor is your best bet. Why? He/she knows your history and works with you over time to manage your overall health.
Can’t wait to see your doc?

Teladoc® (24/7 care)

Perfect for minor health issues like cold and flu symptoms, rashes, allergies and much more.

Cost: Generally less than in-person visits.
Wait time: Shortest

Convenience clinic

Retail clinics (in major retail stores) may be less expensive. But, they only treat certain conditions—so check the menu of services before you go.

Cost: Low
Wait time: Generally short

Kohl’s Wellness Centers

Kohl’s offers on-site clinics in several locations to make it easy—and less expensive—to get non-emergency care. Visit your on-site clinic for a wide range of services, including preventive care, vaccinations, care for minor illnesses and more.
Need care now?

Urgent care

Urgent, but not life-threatening? An urgent care clinic is a great solution for minor illnesses, X-rays, sprains, etc.

Cost: Higher
Wait time: 1-2 hours

Emergency room

If your condition or concern is life-threatening, always call 911 and/or go directly to the nearest emergency room.

Cost: Highest
Wait time: Depends on your condition; longest for less serious issues.
