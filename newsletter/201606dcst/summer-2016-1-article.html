5 actually fun (and cheap) date ideas

“Date night.” That thing we’re supposed to do with our spouse or partner instead of sitting home every night in sweatpants. Except…date night feels like added pressure, and sweatpants feel so good.

What if we end up bickering over where to eat? Is the trouble of finding a babysitter worth it if we pass out during the movie? By the time we pay for the sitter, dinner and a cab, it’s going to cost so much...

With a bit of planning (and some helpful resources), date night doesn’t have to be a downer. Here are some ideas:

Get active (or just chill) outdoors

Throw a few snacks and your favorite Frisbee (or soccer ball or whatever) into a bag, download some podcasts or a new album, and head to a nearby park. In the Milwaukee area? Check out these 8 great picnic spots. Catch up and people-watch, get a little competitive, or just savor time away from the kids (or dogs, or parents, or work)...

Volunteer together

Choose one of your favorite local organizations, like an animal shelter or museum, and sign up to give back to your community. Even if it’s not your thing, it’d be worth the cute pics, right? Prefer something already set up? See your Kohl’s Cares captain for Kohl’s volunteer events in your area.

Discount movie night

Most reduced-price ticket nights are on a weekday, but hey, sometimes a mid-week escape is exactly what you need. Marcus Theaters has $5 new release movies on Tuesday nights. And don’t forget to check Kohl’s Perks for deals on movie tickets, theme parks and more.

Free music, al fresco

Spring and summer mean outdoor tunes! Check social media or your local paper for live events and free concerts. If you’re in the Milwaukee area, try Jazz in the Park or the Tosa Tonight Series.

Win big!

Well, at least just cheer hard for your team, winning or not. Get discount tickets to different sporting events through Kohl’s Perks.
