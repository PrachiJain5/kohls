There’s an app for that!

Next time you’re in the doctor’s office or at the pharmacy trying to recall some bit of info you need asap, just pull up any of these free apps.

Most of them provide you all the basics you’d expect—your plan details, coverage info, doc finders, claims history and status, prescription history and appointments—but they can do a lot more, too. Download them today to use in a pinch tomorrow.
