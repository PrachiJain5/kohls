Budgeting: A love/hate relationship

When you set out to create a budget, you might feel stressed (or sleepy). Staring at a bunch of numbers, especially those about your money habits, isn’t exactly everyone’s idea of a good time. Plus, keeping a budget makes you painfully aware of what you shouldn’t be spending your money on, like that daily latte. But, deep down we all know a budget only has our best interests at heart!

Let’s be honest here, budgeting can be a real pain, but it pays off—literally. Slowly but surely, you realize the small sacrifices ARE worth it. Like when you can finally take that vacation you’ve been dreaming about...for years.
