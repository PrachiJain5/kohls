What’s New for 2018?

Kohl’s offers best-in-class medical, dental and vision coverage—as well as flexible spending accounts, the Hyatt Legal Plan and the Vacation Buy Program. And, don’t forget the Accident Protection and Critical Illness Plans, which can provide extra financial support beyond what your medical plan covers. It’s up to you to first choose the right benefits, then use them wisely throughout the year.

While there aren’t many changes for next year, here are some important updates for 2018:

Medical Plan Changes

Be sure to open and read each section below to learn what’s new for 2018.

All Plans

New tiered networks—To help you get the best value from your medical coverage, we’re introducing a new approach to in-network care for 2018.

UnitedHealthcare

You’ll get the highest level of coverage for all services by using in-network doctors that are recognized as top-rated for both quality and cost-effectiveness.

UnitedHealthcare designates its top-rated primary and specialty doctors as Tier 1 physicians. When you use a UHC Tier 1 doctor, you’ll pay 20% after deductible. For all other in-network doctors, you’ll pay 30% after deductible.

Look for the Tier 1 designation when you search for in-network providers on UHC’s website or on UHC’s Health4Me app.

What about Anthem?

If you’re covered through Anthem, most in-network services will continue to be covered at 80% after you meet the annual deductible.

However, if you need care for certain specialized conditions, you’ll receive higher benefits for using Anthem’s network of top-rated facilities for those conditions. The concept works much like UHC’s Tier 1 network, but only applies to a few services in 2018.

Anthem designates its highly rated facilities—measured on both quality of care and cost-efficiency standards—as Blue Distinction Centers +.

When you use one of these facilities for bariatric surgery,* cardiac care, knee/hip replacement or spine surgery, you’ll pay 20% after deductible at a Blue Distinction Center + facility. If you use a Blue Distinction Center or another in-network facility* instead, you’ll pay 30% after deductible.

Transplants must be performed at either an Anthem Blue Distinction Center + or Blue Distinction Center facility to be covered. For bariatric surgery or transplants, you’ll pay 20% after deductible for care received from one of these facilities.

Look for the Blue Distinction Center + designation when you search for in-network facilities at anthem.com or on the Anthem Anywhere app.

* Bariatric surgery must be performed at either a Blue Distinction Center + or Blue Distinction Center facility to be covered.

Additional Basic Plan Changes

  • Separate in-network and out-of-network deductibles and out-of-pocket maximums—If you use both in-network and out-of-network providers, you will have to meet two separate deductibles and out-of-pocket maximums.

    This means that out-of-network charges will not count toward the in-network annual deductible or out-of-pocket maximum. The same goes for in-network charges—they will not count toward the out-of-network annual deductible or out-of-pocket maximum.

  • The annual deductible will increase to $2,000 single and $4,000 family.
  • The annual out-of-pocket maximum will increase to $6,000 single and $12,000 family.
  • The per-person out-of-pocket maximum will increase to $7,300; once someone reaches the per-person maximum in a year, the plan pays 100% of that person’s remaining eligible expenses for the year.
  • The Teladoc per-visit copay will increase to $45.

Additional Savers Plan Changes

  • Separate in-network and out-of-network deductibles and out-of-pocket maximums—If you use both in-network and out-of-network providers, you will have to meet two separate deductibles and out-of-pocket maximums.

    This means that out-of-network charges will not count toward the in-network annual deductible or out-of-pocket maximum. The same goes for in-network charges—they will not count toward the out-of-network annual deductible or out-of-pocket maximum.

  • The annual deductible will increase to $1,500 single and $3,000 family.
  • The Teladoc per-visit copay will increase to $45.

Additional Exclusive Provider Plan Changes

  • Office visit copays will increase to $30 for primary care and $45 for a specialist.
  • There is a new family deductible of $1,200.

Changes to Teladoc as a Voluntary Benefit

  • The per-visit copay will increase to $45.
