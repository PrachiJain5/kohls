Enroll in 2018 Benefits

within 45 days of your date of hire or status change

When it comes to choosing your Kohl’s benefits, you're in charge. It’s your choice. They’re your benefits. And, it’s your life. And, it all starts with enrollment. So now’s the time to choose well.

You must enroll within 45 days of your date of hire, status change or life event if you wish to have coverage for 2018.

This guide has everything you need to know to enroll in your 2018 benefits, plus some tips to help you get more out of your coverage year-round. Be sure to review all the info that’s covered—now and throughout the year!

Required Now

If you are newly eligible for coverage, have had a status change or have experienced a life event, you must enroll for these benefits within 45 days of your date of hire, status change or life event.

  • Telemedicine (Teladoc®)
  • Dental
  • Vision
  • Flexible Spending Accounts (FSAs)
  • Hyatt Legal Plan

If you don’t enroll within 45 days, you won’t be able to make changes until Open Enrollment, unless you experience a qualifying event (like marriage or the birth or adoption of a child).

Optional Now

You may enroll for these benefits within 45 days of your date of hire or status change, if you’re newly eligible for coverage.

  • Life Insurance
  • Dependent Life Insurance

Note: Unless you’re enrolling when you’re newly eligible for benefits, you must complete and submit an evidence of insurability (EOI) form to request approval to elect Life and Dependent Life Insurance for the first time. Your enrollment is contingent upon approval through Securian Life. You’ll receive more information after enrollment about how to complete this process.

After 45 days, you won’t be able to make changes until Open Enrollment, unless you experience a qualifying event (like marriage or the birth or adoption of a child).

Optional Anytime

You can elect or change these benefits any time:

  • Kohl’s 401(k) Savings Plan*
  • Commuter Program
  • Identity Theft Protection
  • Pet Insurance
  • Auto and Home Insurance

*Must work 1,000 hours in a calendar year to be eligible.
