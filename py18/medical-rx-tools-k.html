About Consumer-Driven Health Plans (CDHPs)

The Consumer-Driven Health Plans—the Savers Plan and Basic Plan—cover the same services as the Kaiser Plan, but offer:

  • Lower costs per paycheck than the Kaiser Plan;
  • A Health Savings Account (HSA) to which you (and Kohl’s, if enrolled in the Savers Plan) can contribute tax-free money to help you pay for certain health care costs; and
  • Out-of-network benefits—Unlike the Kaiser Plan, these plans offer out-of-network coverage, which may come in handy if you cover family members living outside the network area (such as a dependent in college).
