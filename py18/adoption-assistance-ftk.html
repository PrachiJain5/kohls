If You’re Planning to Adopt, Kohl’s Can Help.

Kohl’s reimburses up to $10,000 per adoption for both domestic and foreign adoption expenses, with certain requirements. Eligible expenses include:

  • Attorney fees and court costs
  • Adoption fees
  • Transportation costs associated with the adoption

You will receive up to four weeks of paid time off for each adoption, with a maximum of four weeks in a rolling 12-month period.

The EAP also provides adoption research and resources at no cost to you.
