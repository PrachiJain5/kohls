Why Get Dental Checkups?

Maybe you’re not worried about cavities anymore, but regular dental visits and preventive care are just as important for adults as they are for children. Dental exams are key to early detection and staying ahead of problems like gum disease, which can also have an impact on your overall health.

Untreated gum and other dental disease can lead to serious problems like infection, nerve and bone damage, and even other health issues like heart disease, stroke and diabetes. So take advantage of preventive dental benefits—they’re covered at 100% when you use a Delta Dental PPO Network dentist.
